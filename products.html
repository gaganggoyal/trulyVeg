The Directory

Verified truly-veg products

Every product below passed our three checks: ingredients (nothing animal-derived, nothing synthetic-passed-as-food), process (no animal-derived processing aids), and sourcing (traceable origin). Each card tells you what to look for on the label and where to buy — online and offline.

Kitchen Staples✅ Truly Veg

Naturally Fermented Sugarcane Vinegar (Sirka)

Made by slow-fermenting sugarcane juice — first to alcohol, then to vinegar — exactly how Indian sirka has been made for centuries. Cloudy, aromatic, alive.

Look for“Naturally fermented / brewed” on the label; slight cloudiness or a “mother” is a good sign
Avoid“Vinegar” with just “acetic acid + water” in ingredients — that’s synthetic
Typical price₹90–₹250 / 500 ml
⚠️ Many big-brand “white vinegars” are diluted synthetic acetic acid. If the label doesn’t say fermented/brewed, assume it isn’t.
Amazon / BigBasketOrganic storesLocal sirka makers (UP, Punjab)
Kitchen Staples✅ Truly Veg

Raw Apple Cider Vinegar (with the Mother)

Fermented from whole apples; the “mother” is the living culture of the fermentation. A versatile swap for synthetic vinegar in salads, pickles and drinks.

Look for“Raw, unfiltered, with mother”; ingredient list should say only apple juice/cider
AvoidCrystal-clear “ACV-flavoured” vinegar; “ACV with honey” if you avoid honey
Typical price₹250–₹600 / 500 ml
Amazon / FlipkartHealth-food storesBrand websites
Kitchen Staples✅ Truly Veg

Jaggery (Gur) & Khandsari Sugar

Minimally processed sugarcane sweeteners that keep their minerals. Also sidesteps the bone-char filtration used on refined white sugar in some countries (notably parts of the US).

Look forDark, chemical-free gur; “sulphurless” khandsari; organic certification
AvoidBright golden-yellow jaggery — often treated with sodium hydrosulphite
Typical price₹60–₹180 / kg
Local mandi / kiranaBigBasket / JioMartFarmer collectives
Kitchen Staples✅ Truly Veg

Pure Desi Ghee (Bilona / A2)

Ghee made from cultured butter (bilona method) is a sattvic staple praised in Ayurveda. The concern isn’t veg status — it’s adulteration with vanaspati or animal fats in loose/cheap ghee.

Look forAGMARK certification; single ingredient: “cow ghee/butter”; granular texture, nutty aroma
AvoidUnbranded loose ghee; suspiciously cheap “ghee” (pure ghee cannot cost less than butter)
Typical price₹600–₹1,800 / 500 ml (bilona A2 at the top end)
Trusted dairiesAmazon / brand sitesLocal gaushala brands
Kitchen Staples⚠️ Check Label

Silver Leaf (Chandi Varq) — Machine-Made Only

The shiny varq on sweets was traditionally beaten between layers of animal hide or intestine. Modern machine-made varq uses food-grade polyester sheets — genuinely vegetarian.

Look for“Machine-made / vegetarian varq”; FSSAI requires varq made without animal material
AvoidUnbranded varq from unknown sources, especially on loose sweets
⚠️ At a sweet shop, ask how their varq is made. If they don’t know, choose sweets without it.
Branded sweet chainsBaking supply stores
Kitchen Staples⚠️ Check Label

Saffron (Kesar) — Certified & Traceable

Real saffron is a flower stigma — perfectly veg. The market problem is fakes: dyed corn silk, coloured threads, or synthetic colourants sold as kesar.

Look forGI-tagged Kashmiri saffron; ISO 3632 grading; trumpet-shaped threads that release colour slowly
AvoidCheap “saffron” that stains water instantly deep-red (sign of synthetic dye)
Typical price₹300–₹500 / gram for genuine Kashmiri
GI-tagged brandsGovt. emporiumsAmazon (brand stores)
Dairy & Cheese✅ Truly Veg

Vegetarian-Rennet Cheese (Microbial / FPC)

Cheese set with microbial rennet or fermentation-produced chymosin instead of calf-stomach rennet. Most Indian-made cheese is vegetarian; imported hard cheeses often are not.

Look for“Microbial rennet / vegetarian rennet / non-animal enzymes”; green dot on Indian packs
AvoidTraditional PDO Parmigiano-Reggiano, Grana Padano, Gorgonzola — animal rennet is mandatory in their recipe
⚠️ “Enzymes” with no source named on an imported cheese = assume animal rennet.
Indian cheese brandsGourmet stores (ask!)BigBasket
Dairy & Cheese✅ Truly Veg

Fresh Paneer & Homemade Chenna

India’s original vegetarian cheese — milk split with lemon juice, curd or citric acid. No rennet, no additives, no doubt. Best made fresh at home in 20 minutes.

Look forSame-day paneer from a trusted dairy; or make it: 1L milk + 2 tbsp lemon juice
Avoid“Analogue paneer” (starch + vegetable oil) sold cheap to caterers
Typical price₹300–₹450 / kg (genuine milk paneer)
Local dairyMake at homeMilk-brand outlets
Dairy & Cheese⚠️ Check Label

Curd & Yogurt (Live Cultures)

Dahi set with bacterial cultures is fully vegetarian. Watch flavoured and “set” commercial yogurts — some use gelatin for texture, and fruit-on-the-bottom versions may use carmine (E120) for pink colour.

Look forIngredients: milk + cultures, nothing else; pectin or starch as thickener is fine
AvoidGelatin in ingredients; E120/carmine in strawberry-pink yogurts
Any dairy brand (plain)Set at home overnight
Snacks & Bakery✅ Truly Veg

Agar-Agar Jelly & Desserts (China Grass)

Seaweed-based setting agent that makes jelly, panna cotta and falooda without gelatin. Sets at room temperature and holds firmer than gelatin.

Look for“Agar-agar / china grass” strands or powder; jelly crystals explicitly marked vegetarian
AvoidImported jelly crystals and marshmallows without a veg mark — usually gelatin
Typical price₹80–₹200 / 50 g
Baking storesAmazon / BigBasketChinese grocery aisles
Snacks & Bakery⚠️ Check Label

Bread & Buns — Without E920 (L-Cysteine)

Softening agent E920 can be sourced from poultry feathers or hair. Many artisan and branded breads are clean — the ingredient list tells you.

Look forShort ingredient lists: flour, water, yeast, salt, oil; “vegan” certified breads are always safe
Avoid“Improver (E920)” or unnamed “flour treatment agents” on soft burger buns
Artisan bakeriesSourdough makersBake at home
Snacks & Bakery⚠️ Check Label

Chips & Instant Noodles — Clean Seasoning

Flavour enhancers E627/E631 (disodium guanylate/inosinate) can be made from fish or meat extract — or from plant/microbial sources. In India the green dot resolves it; abroad, it doesn’t.

Look forGreen-dot marked packs in India; explicitly vegetarian/vegan flavours abroad
Avoid“Chicken-flavour” anything (often real extract); unlabelled E627/E631 outside India
Green-dot brandsRoasted makhana / homemade
Snacks & Bakery✅ Truly Veg

Eggless Bakery & Aquafaba Meringues

India leads the world in eggless baking. Aquafaba (chickpea brine) whips like egg white; flaxseed gel binds like egg. Cakes, macarons and meringues — fully veg.

Look for“100% eggless” bakeries; check icing for gelatin-based stabilisers in fondant
AvoidMarshmallow fondant and mousse cakes without a veg mark (gelatin)
Eggless bakeriesHome bakersDIY aquafaba
Health & Supplements✅ Truly Veg

Plant-Based Vitamin D3 (Lichen-Derived)

Standard D3 supplements are made from lanolin (sheep’s wool grease) or fish liver oil. Lichen-derived D3 delivers the same cholecalciferol from a plant source.

Look for“Vegan D3 / lichen-derived cholecalciferol” on the label
AvoidD3 with unnamed source + gelatin softgel — double non-veg
Typical price₹400–₹900 / 60 tablets
Pharmacies (ask)Amazon / 1mgVegan supplement brands
Health & Supplements✅ Truly Veg

Algal Omega-3 (DHA + EPA)

Fish don’t make omega-3 — the algae they eat do. Algal oil supplements go straight to the source: same DHA/EPA, no fish, no ocean burden, no fishy aftertaste.

Look for“Algal oil / Schizochytrium sp.” with stated DHA+EPA mg; veg capsule shell
Avoid“Omega-3” fish-oil softgels (fish + gelatin shell); flaxseed alone gives ALA, not DHA
Typical price₹700–₹1,500 / 30 capsules
Amazon / HealthKartVegan brands
Health & Supplements✅ Truly Veg

Veg Capsules (HPMC / Pullulan Shells)

Most capsule shells are gelatin — even on herbal and “ayurvedic” products. HPMC (plant cellulose) and pullulan (fermented tapioca) capsules carry the same medicine, truly veg.

Look for“Veg capsule / HPMC / cellulose capsule” printed on the pack; green dot in India
Avoid“Hard gelatin capsule” — printed in small text on many strips
⚠️ Ask your pharmacist for tablet or veg-capsule versions of the same medicine — they usually exist.
Pharmacies (ask)Brands with veg-caps range
Health & Supplements✅ Truly Veg

Plant Protein (Pea, Brown Rice, Soy)

Complete protein without whey processing doubts or non-veg additives. Pea + rice blends match dairy protein’s amino profile; classic Indian option: sattu and roasted chana.

Look forShort labels; stated protein %; third-party lab-tested brands
AvoidProteins with “fish collagen” blends; unverified imported jars
Typical price₹1,200–₹2,500 / kg — or ₹120/kg for sattu!
Amazon / HealthKartSattu from kirana
Colours & Setting Agents✅ Truly Veg

Plant-Based Food Colours

Beetroot (pink-red), turmeric (yellow), annatto (orange), butterfly pea (blue), spinach (green) — every colour carmine gives you, from plants instead of crushed insects.

Look for“Natural colour: beetroot extract / curcumin / annatto (E160b)”
AvoidE120 / carmine / cochineal / “natural red 4” — all insect-derived
Baking supply storesAmazonYour spice box
Colours & Setting Agents✅ Truly Veg

Fruit Pectin (Jams & Gummies)

The setting agent in traditional jams — extracted from apple pomace and citrus peel. Modern veg gummies (including vitamins) use pectin instead of gelatin.

Look for“Pectin (E440)” — always plant-derived; “pectin-based gummy”
AvoidGummies listing “gelatin” — most imported gummy bears and gummy vitamins
Baking storesAmazon
Colours & Setting Agents✅ Truly Veg

Carnauba Wax Glaze (Instead of Shellac)

The shine on candies and apples is often shellac (E904) — secreted by lac insects. Carnauba wax (E903), from Brazilian palm leaves, does the same job from a tree.

Look for“Glazing agent: carnauba wax (E903)”
Avoid“Confectioner’s glaze / shellac / E904” on candy and chocolate coatings
Check candy labelsUnwaxed local fruit
