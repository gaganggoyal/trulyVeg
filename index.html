Don’t eat synthetic products thinking they’re “veg”.

Synthetic vinegar is diluted industrial acetic acid — not food fermented from food. Carmine (E120) red is made from crushed insects; the bright azo dyes are born from petroleum; and on loose sweets, illegal industrial colours hide with no label at all. E920 in bread can come from feathers. “Veg” and “natural” are not the same word — and neither is guaranteed by a green dot. Read the full Hidden Non-Veg List →

Most cheap “white vinegar” is industrial acetic acid diluted with water — it was never food. Naturally fermented vinegar (sugarcane, apple cider, coconut, jamun) is made from real produce, slowly, the way it has been for centuries.

Instead ofGelatin (Jelly, Gummies, Capsules)

ChooseAgar-Agar (China Grass) & Pectin

Gelatin is boiled-down animal skin, bones and connective tissue — and it hides in jelly, marshmallows, gummy vitamins and capsule shells. Agar-agar (from seaweed) and pectin (from fruit) set desserts beautifully and are 100% plant.

ChooseVegetarian-Rennet Cheese & Paneer

Traditional rennet is an enzyme taken from the stomach lining of slaughtered calves — used in many imported parmesan, cheddar and gouda. Microbial-rennet cheese and fresh paneer give you the same joy, truly veg.
