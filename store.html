The TrulyVeg Promise

Why buying here will mean something

Ingredients, process and sourcing audited for every SKU — and the audit notes published on the product page. No “trust us”; read it yourself.

Questions

Store FAQ

Will everything be vegetarian?

Strictly. And beyond that — everything will pass the “truly food” test too: no synthetic passing as natural, anywhere in the store. Jain-friendly and vegan filters will be built in from day one.

Why should I trust your verification?

Because it’s published. Every product page will show what we checked, when, and what the lab said. If a batch fails, we’ll say so publicly and pull it.

Will the free guide remain free?

Forever. The directory, Swap Guide, and Hidden Non-Veg List are the mission; the store funds them.

What does the waitlist get me?

Beta access before public launch, founding-member pricing locked for a year, and a vote on which products we stock next.

Do you ship outside India?

Not at launch — international shipping follows in phase two. Waitlist members abroad will be first to know.
